Assignment 1: Cross-Platform Procrastinating

0 Possible Points

Details

In a world full of never-ending work and assignments, we are asked for our non-existant responsibilities that makes us sleep deprived and less suprisingly stressed. What would we, procrastinators, look like in 2022?

In this short project, we'll play with blissfully traveling through links to effectively procrastinate before we start our actual assignments. Your project will begin by staring into the computer for 5 minutes or going on Youtube with your phone until you feel like working. What can you do to procrastinate as much as possible?

Notes:

  • To begin with find the comment made by "due tmr do tmr" and click the channel
  • You must follow the instructions to follow through the links
  • Feel free to get sidetracked as much as you want
